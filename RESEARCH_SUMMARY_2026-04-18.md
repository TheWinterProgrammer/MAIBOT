## Strategic Vulnerabilities & Supply Chain Risks

### Critical Dependencies

**Microelectronics (Strategic Vulnerability):**
- Still import-dependent (primarily China)
- If export controls tighten → mass production significantly disrupted
- Requires: Certified clean-room facilities, massive investment
- Timeline: Years to achieve independence

**Advanced Optics:**
- Import-dependent for precision optics
- Localization efforts ongoing but not yet at scale
- Requires: Specialized manufacturing and testing facilities

**Manufacturing Infrastructure:**
- Risk: Ongoing shelling and infrastructure damage (e.g., Mukachevo SMT line destroyed)
- Mitigation: Distributed 76+ contractor network provides redundancy

### Localization Roadmap

**Short-term (Achieved):**
- Frames, propellers, ESCs, basic electronics, thermal modules

**Medium-term (2025-2026):**
- Flight controllers, motors, video transmitters

**Long-term (Strategic Challenge):**
- Microelectronics fabrication
- Advanced optics manufacturing
- Semiconductor independence
