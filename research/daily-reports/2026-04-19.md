**Status:** Full marketplace integration as of 2026
- Military units can now independently select and order ground robotic systems
- Direct ordering through standard procedures with allocated budgets
- Previously available only under "Army of Drones Bonus" program
- **YTD 2026 allocation:** Nearly UAH 9 billion for orders

> "The nature of warfare is evolving. There is increasing demand for capabilities that enable missions without exposing personnel to the strike zone. Ground robotic systems are used for evacuation, logistics, ammunition delivery, and mission execution."
