## Ukrainian Military Robot Systems

### Officially Codified Systems (Ministry of Defence)

Since the full-scale invasion began, the Ministry of Defence has codified and authorized **more than 80 ground robotic systems** for deployment:
- **2023:** ~10 systems
- **2024:** ~50+ systems  
- **2025 (to date):** ~20+ systems

#### Key Systems Identified

**1. KRAMPUS Mobile Strike System**
- **Role:** Assault/Defensive operations with thermobaric launchers
- **Weapon:** RPV-16 reactive thermobaric launchers (Ukrainian-made)
- **Capabilities:**
  - Two silent electric motors + tracked chassis
  - High mobility: off-road, forests, sand, swamps, steep slopes
  - EW-resistant control channels
  - All-weather operation (cold, heat, snow, rain)
  - Extended battery capacity for hours of continuous movement
  - Video cameras for surveillance
- **Size:** Compact, fits in pickup truck bed or minibus
- **Status:** Codified and authorized by MoD

**2. Droid TW / Droid TW 12.7**
- **Role:** Fire support robot
- **Weapon:** Browning 12.7mm machine gun
- **AI Capabilities:** AI-powered autonomous operation
- **Platform:** Tracked
- **Status:** Codified and combat-tested

**3. NUMO (Tank Bureau)**
- **Role:** Multi-purpose cargo/logistics
- **Capacity:** Up to 300 kg payload
- **Manufacturer:** NUMO Robotics (formerly Tank Bureau)
- **Status:** Codified by Ministry of Defence
- **Production:** 600+ platforms manufactured, 100+ person team

**4. UNEX (Ukrainian-Czech)**
- **Role:** Amphibious unmanned platform
- **Capabilities:** Traverses swamps, ice, rocky terrain, can swim
- **Control:** Up to 10,000m via radio; unlimited via LTE (GSM)
- **Status:** Approved for Defence Forces

**5. Bufalo**
- **Role:** Combat UGV
- **Capabilities:** Armored, AI-equipped
- **Range:** Up to 200 km
- **Status:** Currently in testing phase
