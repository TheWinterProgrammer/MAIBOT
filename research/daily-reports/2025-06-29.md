## 🆕 New Developments

### Ministry of Defence Codifications (June 2025)

#### 1. DODGER UGV
- **Status:** Officially codified and authorized by Ministry of Defence
- **Type:** Multi-purpose unmanned ground robotic system
- **Payload:** 250 kg
- **Key Features:**
  - Armored plating against small arms fire and shrapnel
  - Compact, low-profile design
  - Developed with frontline soldier input
- **Missions:** Logistics, evacuation, combat engineering, mine deployment
- **Source:** [mod.gov.ua](https://mod.gov.ua/en/news/bulletproof-and-versatile-ukraines-arsenal-of-ground-robotic-systems-has-been-reinforced-with-a-new-addition)

#### 2. TERMIT UGV
- **Status:** Codified and approved for Armed Forces use
- **Developer:** Tencore (Ukrainian company)
- **Investment:** $3.74 million from US-Ukraine MITS Capital
- **Type:** Tracked modular platform
- **Capabilities:** Logistics, evacuation, engineering, fire support, surveillance
- **Notable:** Can be equipped with Browning machine guns or Starlink terminals
- **Source:** [Cyber Drone Solutions](https://cyberdronesolutions.com/platforms/termit/)

## ✈️ Aerial Systems

### Litavr Interceptor Drone
- **Developer:** F-Drones (Ukrainian)
- **Status:** In Brave1 restricted catalog since Autumn 2025
- **Max Speed:** 350 km/h
- **Operational Radius:** 40 km (recorded max: 80+ km)
- **Max Altitude:** 9,000 meters
- **Key Feature:** Automatic terminal guidance (Last Mile/Pixel Lock)
- **Control:** Remote module allows operator control from hundreds/thousands of km away
- **Warhead:** Two variants - empty container for on-site loading, or NATO-coded ready-made
- **Components:** Self-developed software, electronics, engines, speed regulators, flight controllers
- **Development Timeline:**
  - Autumn 2024: Development start
  - Summer 2025: Successful testing
  - Autumn 2025: Serial production and supply
- **Source:** [Interfax Ukraine](https://en.interfax.com.ua/news/general/1175110.html)

## 🔧 Component Manufacturing Analysis

### Domestic Production Achievements

#### Motor-G (Largest Ukrainian Motor Manufacturer)
- **Production Capacity:** Approaching 100,000 motors/month
- **Production Start:** December 2024
- **Products:** FPV motors, fiber-optic drone motors (up to 15" frames)
- **Status:** Likely largest in Ukraine and Europe

#### Flight Controllers (Ukrainian-Made)
1. **Flytex Fuzia F405 v2** + ESC 65A 6S V4
2. **603700 GALYCHYNA F405 v2** + FRANKIVSK v2 6S/60A ESC

### Components Still Dependent on China
- Optical fiber cables (Hubei province)
- Chemical manufacturing equipment
- Advanced machining tools
- Magnets for motor production
- Some thermal vision components

### Component Independence Statistics
- **95%** of Ukrainian defense industry members still use Chinese components
- **28%** do not use Taiwanese components
- **40%** do not use US components
- **Risk assessment:** Pentagon analysis warns China could shut down global drone industry for a year

## 🎯 Key Insights

1. **Institutional Integration:** Ukraine is systematically integrating robotic units into combat brigades, not just deploying ad-hoc systems

2. **Domestic Manufacturing Push:** Significant investment in component independence, particularly motors and flight controllers

3. **AI Integration:** Litavr's autonomous terminal guidance represents advancement in AI-guided systems

4. **Modular Design Philosophy:** TERMIT and Ratel platforms emphasize modularity for multi-mission capability

5. **Supply Chain Risk Awareness:** Clear recognition of dependence risks, active efforts to localize production
