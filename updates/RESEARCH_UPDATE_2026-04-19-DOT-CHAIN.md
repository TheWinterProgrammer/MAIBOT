## Ground Robotic Systems Integration

### Platform Expansion
As of early 2026, the Ministry of Defence has enabled direct ordering of **ground robotic systems** through DOT-Chain Defence:

- **7 models** from **6 Ukrainian manufacturers** now available
- Units can independently select and order with allocated budgets
- Previously available only under "Army of Drones Bonus" program
- **YTD 2026 allocation:** Nearly UAH 9 billion for DOT-Chain orders

### Available Ground Systems

#### КНРО «Змій» (Zmiy Sapper)
- **Type:** Explosive Ordnance Disposal Robot
- **Capability:** Explosion-resistant, rapid demining
- **Availability:** Brave1 Market

#### TerMIT Universal UGV
- **Type:** Universal tracked ground robot
- **Use Cases:** Evacuation, logistics, firepower application
- **Availability:** DOT-Chain Defence / Brave1 Market

**Arsen Zhumadilov, Director of DOT:**
> "Ground robotic systems are used for evacuation, logistics, ammunition delivery, and mission execution. Through the system, the military can independently order the required assets within their allocated budgets."

### System Workflow
1. Military uploads mission completion videos to DELTA system
2. Brave1 verifies materials and awards e-Points
3. Units use e-Points to order equipment on Brave1 Market
4. DOT-Chain Defence negotiates, pays, and handles logistics

### Six-Month Performance Summary
- **240,000+ drones** ordered via marketplace
- Ground UGVs, components, and combat equipment included
- 2026 expansion to additional Defense Forces units planned
